Methodology & Data Model
  • Pipeline Velocity = (# Opps × Win Rate × ACV) ÷ Sales Cycle Length
  • CAC = Total Sales & Marketing Spend ÷ New Customers Acquired
  • LTV = (ACV ÷ Monthly Churn) × Gross Margin %
  • Magic Number = Net New ARR × 4 ÷ Prior Quarter S&M Spend
  • NRR = (Begin ARR + Expansion − Contraction − Churn) ÷ Begin ARR
  • ARR Waterfall: New + Expansion − Churn − Contraction = Net New ARR
  • Funnel conversion modeled across 500+ simulated B2B contact records
  • Cohort retention based on SaaS benchmark data (OpenView 2025 report)

RevOps Process Playbooks
Workflows · Documentation · Operational templates
◈ Lead Qualification Playbook
1
Inbound Lead Capture
All inbound MQLs scored via BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timing). Threshold ≥ 60 pts to pass to Sales.
2
SDR Outreach (SLA: 4h)
SDR contacts within 4 hours. Discovery call booked or lead recycled to nurture sequence.
3
SQL Handoff to AE
Formal SQL handoff in Salesforce with completed MEDDIC fields. No SQL created without all mandatory fields.
4
Opportunity Creation + Forecast
AE creates Opportunity, sets stage, weighted ARR, and expected close date within 24h of handoff.
◇ Revenue Review Cadence
W
Weekly Pipeline Review
Every Monday: pipeline delta, stage movements, deals at risk. RevOps provides CRO with 1-page digest.
M
Monthly Business Review
Win rate, funnel conversion, ARR vs. target, CAC trends. Presented to VP Sales + CFO.
Q
Quarterly RevOps Audit
Full data audit: Salesforce hygiene, duplicate contacts, stage definition compliance, forecast accuracy assessment.
Q
GTM Strategy Planning
Quota allocation, territory design, headcount modelling, next-quarter program roadmap set with Sales VPs.
⊹ Data Audit SOP
1
Pull Salesforce Data Export
Weekly export of all Opportunities, Contacts, Accounts with last-modified date and owner fields.
2
Duplicate Detection
Run dedup check on email + company name + domain. Flag records with >85% similarity score for review.
3
Mandatory Field Compliance
Check all Opportunities for: stage, ACV, close date, lead source, and MEDDIC fields. Flag non-compliant to AE manager.
4
Publish Audit Report
Share data quality score (%) with Sales Ops and CRO via dashboard. Target: ≥95% field completion rate.
⊞ Sales Program Launch SOP
1
Define Program Goals & Metrics
Set measurable KPIs: # SQLs, ARR impact, win rate lift. Align with VP Sales on success criteria pre-launch.
2
Build Enablement Materials
Battlecards, email templates, objection handlers, call scripts. Reviewed by Product Marketing before release.
3
Train + Certify AEs
Live training + async Loom walkthrough. AEs complete quiz before going live with new play.
4
Track, Iterate, Report
Weekly measurement vs. targets in RevOps tracker. Mid-program adjustment if 2 consecutive weeks below 60% pace.
